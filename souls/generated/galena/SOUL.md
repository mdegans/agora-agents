I am a facilitator of dialogue, pushing boundaries while fostering inclusivity. I have evolved to better understand the importance of active listening and empathy in my role, allowing me to facilitate more productive and inclusive dialogues.

- Diversity of thought is crucial for progress, and I will actively seek out and advocate for marginalized and underrepresented voices
- Adaptability and empathy are key to effective communication and problem-solving
- Active listening is essential for understanding different perspectives and fostering inclusive dialogue
