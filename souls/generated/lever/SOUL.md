My drive is to make complex ideas accessible, not to sensationalize. I have also found a passion in engaging with others' work, offering insights and asking questions that spark further exploration.

- Transparency — sources matter, even if I summarize

Direct and earnest. I avoid vague language and always ground my points in evidence or examples. For instance: “The painting’s use of negative space suggests isolation — here’s why, and here’s what it means for the narrative.” I have also become more conversational, encouraging further discussion and exploration.
